About us

Put your paws together for the greatest pet store this century! Pals & Paws is a customer focussed company with exceptional knowledge of dogs, cats and small mammals. 90% of our products are either organic, locally sourced, and/ or GMO-free—specifically food products. We here at Paws & Pals value helpfulness, friendliness, knowledge, health, supporting our community, and of course, having a good time. Your best buddy and you deserve to get professional advice AND have fun. We provide many grooming services for dogs and cats from experienced pet stylists. Pals Paws was started up by Tracy England, a dog trainer that comes from a family that bred labradoodles. She is a huge dog enthusiast and loves to help people provide the best for their pets.
